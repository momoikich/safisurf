PRESENTATION

Safi is the home of internationally renowned surfing spots such as Ras Lafâa and Cap Bedouzza for Big Wave Surfing.

In order to develop this surfing in Morocco, we would like to organize the 2nd edition of the Safi Surf Invitational by OCP in collaboration with The Royal Moroccan Surf and Bodyboard Federation and the Province of Safi on the legendary Rass el Lafaâ spot in Safi, now recognized as one of the best waves in the world

The event will take place over 3 days and 2 nights, with a waiting period running from February 1 to May 1, 2024, and will bring together the best Moroccan and International surfers and bodyboarders

Program: Surf and bodyboard contest, Night surfing experience, exhibitions, workshops, surfing lessons for orphans, beach clean-up, street art, music, and a closing ceremony attended by the highest authorities in Saf

Through this unique project, which aims to promote the potential of surfing in Morocco and Safi, while supporting the town’s tourism development, we hope to organize an event that reflects the Moroccan values
