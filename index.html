Memory from 1st edition

Safi Surf Invitational

Held on Friday 24 and Saturday 25 March 2023, the 1st edition of the "Safi Surf Invitational" saw the participation of 36 national and international athletes who took part in a sensational day and night contest in the presence of a huge crowd, enthusiasts and professionals who attended a large-scale show.

Live broadcasts on Arriyadia TV and the evening news on 2M TV and Al Aoula were seen by 1,000,000, 4,000,000 and 2,800,000 viewers respectively. Live streaming with 30,000 Internet users. 35 articles in the national and international digital press. Production of 3 videos and sharing of videos by athletes. 500,000 people reached digitally. Free surf lessons for an orphanage. Beach clean-up with Association Bahri.
